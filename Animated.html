Transformers Animated from 2007 is one of the most loved Transformers series, despite very mixed reception when it was first revealed. Characterized by its unique, hyper-stylized character designs by the mind behind the art direction of Teen Titans and Ben 10: Omniverse, Animated is my favorite Transformers series, both in terms of the cartoon and the toyline. The show is a love letter to the franchise, adapting characters from all across its history and remixing them to fit its world, while delivering a fantastic story with the most consistently good cast of characters in any Transformers cartoon in my opinion. The toys are unreal in how well they adapt such stylized design to plastic, and still manage to function excellently as toys.
