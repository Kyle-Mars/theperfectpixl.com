WAR FOR CYBERTRON TRILOGY TRANSFORMERS ANIMATED ROBOTS IN DISGUISE 2001 MAIN GALLERY
The War for Cybertron Trilogy of toylines ran from 2019-2021, and was what was on retail shelves when I started collecting. Themed, as most modern Transformers toylines are, on updated versions of Generation 1 characters, each chapter featured its own unique aesthetic;  Siege featured Cybertronian alternate modes for its characters and inconsistently used battle damage paint.  Earthrise went back to basics with its designs, delivering clean Earth mode updates to many of the characters Siege also included. Kingdom was a mix of more of Earthrise with toys of characters pulled from Beast Wars. I ended up with a lot of those figures, alongside some I picked up randomly.      Legacy is the spiritual successor to the War for Cybertron Trilogy, using the same design principles and aesthetic and bringing in a more diverse cast of characters from across the Transformers brand. Due to the visual similarities, and the fact that I do not intent to purchase many Legacy figures, there are some lumped in here as well.
